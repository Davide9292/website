Experience Design is so much more than pixels.

Design transcends the mere glow of the screen, thoughtfully embracing both the tangible and the digital realms within their unique contexts. It's the deliberate crafting of moments of wonder – those small sparks of surprise and delight – that have the power to unlock new perspectives, foster deeper understanding, and reveal unforeseen possibilities.

This entire process is fundamentally fueled by a genuine empathy for people, a deep curiosity about their behaviors, and a commitment to designing solutions that truly resonate with their needs and aspirations.

Design is About Designing Experiences in Shared Physical Spaces

I shaped the user experience for Schindler's X8 innovative physical elevator interfaces. My focus on accessibility and ergonomics, driven by in-depth user research and testing with diverse abilities, resulted in a more intuitive, inclusive, and future-ready vertical mobility experience.

Enabling New Work Paradigms with AI

Contributed to defining 5 strategic AI tools for Publicis Groupe, translating complex business needs into tangible concepts in just 4 days. This work unlocked new operational and creative efficiencies, boosting investor confidence and showcasing the impact of AI-driven design on company value.

Bridging Physical & Digital Design

At Habits design studio (2016-2021), I led the experience design for diverse products including wearables, smart home devices, IoT appliances, and mobility solutions. My expertise in interaction and motion design was key in developing innovative HMIs and securing projects with clients like Panasonic, Schindler, Haier, and Braun.

Uplifting engagement and usability

Led the complete, user-centric redesign of the Carrefour Italy B2C app. Through close client collaboration and iterative user testing (from concepts to prototypes), I ensured the new design significantly improved the customer experience and met strategic business goals upon its 2024 launch.

Promoting Accessibility and Consistency

Played a key role in creating WindTre's bespoke Design System. I contributed to mapping and building over 350 foundational components and detailed guidelines, significantly improving accessibility, resolving internal usability conflicts, and enhancing overall user satisfaction across digital platforms.
